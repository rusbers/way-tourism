Greece

In the north of Greece, there is one of the largest complexes of monasteries located on the tops of the cliffs. Its name "Meteora" literally translates as "hanging in the air." This monastery is the main goal of our trip in Greece. After the rocks of sandstone and detrital rock, reaching a height of 600 meters, have been excavated, the indescribable beauty of nature and the atmosphere that reigns in the monasteries of Meteora will be the reward.
